Who We Are

Founded with a love for exceptional coffee, Lebo's Brew Haven is a place where people come together to relax, work, and enjoy handcrafted beverages. Every cup is prepared with carefully selected beans and served with genuine hospitality.

Whether you're stopping by for your morning espresso or meeting friends over a cappuccino, we aim to make every visit memorable.

🎯 Our Mission

To serve high-quality coffee while creating a welcoming environment where everyone feels at home.

🌍 Our Vision

To become Botswana's favourite neighbourhood coffee shop, known for quality, community, and excellent customer service.
